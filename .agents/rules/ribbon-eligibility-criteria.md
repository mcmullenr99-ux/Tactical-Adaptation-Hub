# Ribbon Library Eligibility Criteria

**Last Updated:** 2026-03-27  
**Applies to:** TAG Ribbon Library & Ribbon Bar Builder

---

## Core Rules

All ribbons/medals in the library MUST meet ALL of the following criteria:

### Must Be Strictly Military Issue
- ❌ No Civilian Awards
- ❌ No Police Awards (includes RCMP, NZ Police, AFP overseas medals etc.)
- ❌ No Civilian Medical Staff Awards (e.g. Nursing Service Cross, Royal Red Cross - civilian recipients excluded)
- ❌ No Fire Service / Fire Department Awards
- ❌ No Civilian Emergency Services Awards (e.g. National Emergency Medal AUS)
- ❌ No Civilian Public Service Awards (e.g. Australia Public Service Medal PSM)
- ❌ No Civilian Humanitarian Awards (e.g. Humanitarian Overseas Service Medal)

### Post-1980 Eligibility Rule
- ✅ Campaign medals: only those for operations/service **post 1980**
- ✅ Gallantry/service awards: allowed even if established earlier, **so long as they have been awarded at least once on or after 1980**
- ❌ No WW2 Era medals (last awarded 1946 or earlier)
- ❌ No Korean War Era medals (last awarded ~1954)
- ❌ No Vietnam War Era medals (last awarded 1973)
- ❌ No pre-1980 commemorative medals
- ❌ No medals for operations/service that ended before 1980 with no subsequent awards

### Research & Vetting Requirement
- All medals must be cross-referenced against Google and Wikipedia before being added
- Verify: (a) still awarded after 1980, (b) strictly military recipients, (c) not abolished/obsolete

### Sorting Rule
- In the Ribbon Library & Builder, all ribbons must be sorted into **categories based on purpose**, NOT military branch
- Categories: Gallantry, Orders, Commendations, Campaign Medals, Service Medals, Commemorative (e.g. Jubilee awards), Long Service

## Reference: Removals Made 2026-03-27

The following types were systematically removed during the initial eligibility audit:

**US WW2 era:** American Campaign, Asiatic-Pacific, EAME Campaign, WW2 Victory, American Defense Service, Humane Action (Berlin Airlift), Army/Navy Occupation  
**US Korean War:** Korea Service Medal, Korea Service United Nations, Republic of Korea Service  
**US Vietnam:** Vietnam Service Medal  
**UK:** Korea Medal 1950-53, Vietnam Service Medal 1968-73, Vietnam Logistic Support, OBE (Civil), BEM (Civil)  
**Australia:** Active Service 1945-75, Service Medal 1945-75, GSM Korea, Nursing Service Cross, Police Overseas Service Medal, Public Service Medal, Humanitarian Overseas Service Medal, National Emergency Medal, Civilian Service Medal 1939-45, WW1 Armistice commemoratives  
**Canada:** Canadian Volunteer Service Medal Korea, RCMP Long Service Medal  
**New Zealand:** Service Medal 1946-49, GSM Korea, Special Service Nuclear Testing, Special Service Erebus 1979, Police LS&GC Medal, NZ Commemorative Medal 1990  
**UN:** Korea (UNSER), UNEF (Suez 1956-67), ONUC, UNTEA, UNYOM, UNEFME (all pre-1980)  
**Belgium:** Croix de Guerre 1914-18 & 1940-45, War commemoratives  
**France:** Croix de Guerre 1914-18 & 1939-45, Combattant Volontaire WW1 & Resistance, Médaille Résistance, France Libérée, Médaille commémorative 1939-1945  
**Italy:** Military Order of Savoy (abolished 1947), Order of Crown of Italy (abolished 1946)  
**Greece:** War Cross 1940, Commemorative Medals 1940-41 & 1941-45, Royal Order of George I (monarchy abolished 1973)  
**Norway:** War Medal (WW2), Defense Participation Medal 1940-45, Haakon VII Cross of Liberty, Haakon VII Freedom Medal (all WW2 only)  
**Russia:** Order of St. Vladimir, St. Anne, St. Stanislas (Imperial era, all abolished by Soviets)

## Borderline Decisions (Kept)

- **UK Royal Red Cross (RRC)** — Military nursing decoration, awarded to military nursing personnel (not civilian nurses). Kept as military medical award.
- **UN UNTSO Medal** — Mission ongoing since 1948, still actively awarding today. Kept (post-1980 awards verified).
- **UN UNMOGIP Medal** — Kashmir mission, still ongoing. Kept.
- **France Croix du Combattant** — Still awarded for modern French military combat ops (90 days combat service criterion). Kept.
- **Netherlands Flying Cross** — Still awarded for modern military aviation bravery. Kept.
- **Russia Cross/Medal of St. George** — Reinstated 1992, first modern award 2008 (Russo-Georgian War). Kept.
- **Norway War Cross** — Awarded 2011 for Afghanistan. Kept.
- **Poland Order of Polonia Restituta** — Military division still awarded post-1989. Kept.
- **Australia Southwest Asia Service Medal** — 1990-1995, post-1980. Kept.
